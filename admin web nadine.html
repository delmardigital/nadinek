About Me

Hello,
I'm Nadine

I'm a certified yoga teacher and fitness coach based in Mallorca, with a deep passion for helping people reconnect with their bodies through movement, breath, and mindful touch.

My approach is personal, adaptable, and always centred on you — your needs, your pace, your goals. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced practitioner, I tailor every session to where you are right now.

I travel across all of Mallorca to your home, hotel, or finca, and collaborate with retreats across the Balearic Islands as a guest practitioner.
