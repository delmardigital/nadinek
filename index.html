About Me

Hi, I'm Nadine.

Originally from Germany, I have spent the past 20 years living in Spain, and recently I've begun a new chapter of life in Albania.

A healthy and holistic lifestyle has always been an important part of my life. In 2018 I became a certified yoga teacher. Over the following years I expanded my path as a fitness coach and Thai massage therapist.

Since 2019 I've been leading fun and dynamic women's group workout classes, creating a beautiful community of supportive and inspiring women.

During my recent time in Thailand, I completed my Thai massage certification in Chiang Mai, studying the traditional Northern style.

Why Luna?

Luna means "moon" — a symbol of feminine energy: intuition, compassion, nurturing, creativity, and self-love. Just as the moon moves through its phases, our bodies and energy are constantly shifting and evolving.

Thai Massage

Release, restore &
return to ease.

Rooted in over 2,000 years of tradition, this ancient healing practice combines acupressure, gentle assisted stretches, and rhythmic movements. Performed fully clothed on a comfortable floor mat — grounding and revitalising.

  • Release of tension and stress
  • Improved flexibility and mobility
  • Restored natural energy flow
  • Deep relaxation and wellbeing
